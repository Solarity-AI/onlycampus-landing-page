Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit. They help the site remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how the site is used so we can improve it.

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential operation – Keeping the site working (e.g. security, load balancing, session management)
  • Preferences – Remembering settings such as language or region
  • Analytics – Understanding how visitors use our website so we can improve the experience

Summary of the main types:

  • Strictly necessary – Required for the website to function (e.g. security, basic routing). Session or short-term.
  • Preference – Remember your choices (e.g. language). Up to 1 year.
  • Analytics – Help us see how the site is used so we can improve it. As per our analytics provider.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you block or allow cookies for specific sites. Note that blocking certain cookies may affect how the website works (for example, you may need to log in again or some features may not work as intended).

To learn how to manage cookies in your browser, use the “Help” or “Settings” section of your browser, or search for “cookies” in your browser’s help pages.

We may use similar technologies such as local storage or session storage for preferences and essential functionality. Our use of these is consistent with the purposes described above and our Privacy Policy.

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change when we do. We encourage you to review this page occasionally.

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at support@onlycampus.ai or see our Contact & Support page.

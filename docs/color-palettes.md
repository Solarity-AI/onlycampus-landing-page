## Palette A — "Campus Blue"

**Direction:** Refined blue — academic trust, credibility, and focus.

| Role | HEX | RGB | Preview |
|------|-----|-----|---------|
| **Primary** | `#2563eb` | rgb(37, 99, 235) | ![#2563eb](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/2563eb/2563eb) |
| **Secondary** | `#1e3a5f` | rgb(30, 58, 95) | ![#1e3a5f](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/1e3a5f/1e3a5f) |
| **Accent** | `#38bdf8` | rgb(56, 189, 248) | ![#38bdf8](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/38bdf8/38bdf8) |
| **Background** | `#f8fafc` | rgb(248, 250, 252) | ![#f8fafc](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/f8fafc/f8fafc) |
| **Text** | `#0f172a` | rgb(15, 23, 42) | ![#0f172a](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/0f172a/0f172a) |

### Rationale

Blue is the most recommended color in educational design for its calming, trust-building, and focus-enhancing properties. This palette refines the existing direction with a deeper navy secondary (`#1e3a5f`) that adds institutional gravitas — similar to university branding at schools like Yale and Oxford — while the sky blue accent (`#38bdf8`) introduces youthfulness without sacrificing professionalism. The combination achieves excellent WCAG contrast ratios (~17:1 for text on background), ensuring accessibility across all devices.

**Similar brands:** Coursera, edX, LinkedIn Learning

## Palette B — "Student Energy"

**Direction:** Bold purple + warm orange — creative, youthful, high engagement.

| Role | HEX | RGB | Preview |
|------|-----|-----|---------|
| **Primary** | `#7c3aed` | rgb(124, 58, 237) | ![#7c3aed](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/7c3aed/7c3aed) |
| **Secondary** | `#4e1eec` | rgb(78, 30, 236) | ![#4e1eec](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/4e1eec/4e1eec) |
| **Accent** | `#f97316` | rgb(249, 115, 22) | ![#f97316](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/f97316/f97316) |
| **Background** | `#faf5ff` | rgb(250, 245, 255) | ![#faf5ff](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/faf5ff/faf5ff) |
| **Text** | `#1e1b4b` | rgb(30, 27, 75) | ![#1e1b4b](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/1e1b4b/1e1b4b) |

### Rationale

Purple is associated with wisdom, creativity, and sophistication — ideal for a platform promoting intellectual growth among university students. The vivid orange accent ignites excitement and energy, making it perfect for call-to-action buttons that drive sign-ups and course enrollment. This combination follows the 60-30-10 rule effectively: 60% lavender-white background, 30% purple UI elements, 10% orange on CTAs. The purple-orange pairing is specifically recommended for younger audiences and has proven successful in apps like Notion, Canva, and Kahoot. The lavender-tinted background (`#faf5ff`) keeps the overall feel light and approachable rather than heavy.

**Similar brands:** Notion, Canva, Kahoot, Examarly

## Palette C — "Fresh Campus"

**Direction:** Emerald green + amber — fresh, growth-oriented, modern.

| Role | HEX | RGB | Preview |
|------|-----|-----|---------|
| **Primary** | `#10b981` | rgb(16, 185, 129) | ![#10b981](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/10b981/10b981) |
| **Secondary** | `#0b2d4d` | rgb(11, 45, 77) | ![#0b2d4d](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/0b2d4d/0b2d4d) |
| **Accent** | `#f59e0b` | rgb(245, 158, 11) | ![#f59e0b](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/f59e0b/f59e0b) |
| **Background** | `#f6f8f7` | rgb(246, 248, 247) | ![#f6f8f7](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/f6f8f7/f6f8f7) |
| **Text** | `#1f2a2e` | rgb(31, 42, 46) | ![#1f2a2e](https://via.placeholder.com/60x30/1f2a2e/1f2a2e) |

### Rationale

Green is the most relaxing color for the eyes and represents tranquility, freshness, and harmony — making it ideal for a learning environment. Duolingo's massive success with green-forward branding proves that green works exceptionally well in education: it signals growth, progress, and achievement, exactly what students want to feel. Mint and emerald greens also carry sustainability connotations that resonate strongly with Gen-Z values. The deep teal-navy secondary (`#0b2d4d`) provides the institutional credibility that pure green alone might lack, while the amber accent (`#f59e0b`) creates warm, high-visibility CTAs without clashing with the green primary. This palette positions OnlyCampus as an innovative, forward-thinking platform.

**Similar brands:** Duolingo, Grammarly, Evernote
